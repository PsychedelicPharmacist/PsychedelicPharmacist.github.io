What Is Sophrology?

Sophrology is a structured method created to reveal harmony of body and mind. It employs relaxation, breathing, visualization, gentle movement, and meditation techniques. These practices help activate the body's innate wisdom and healing capabilities while promoting mental and emotional balance.

Body-Mind Integration

Sophrology techniques help create deeper awareness of the mind-body connection, allowing you to tune in to physical sensations and mental states simultaneously.

Conscious Presence

Through guided meditation and awareness practices, sophrology helps bring you fully into the present moment, creating space for insight and healing.

Integration Support

Sophrology offers valuable tools for integrating psychedelic experiences, helping to incorporate insights and transformations into daily life.

How Does Sophrology Work?

Sophrology works by guiding individuals into a state of deep relaxation while maintaining clear awareness. This unique state of consciousness—neither fully awake nor asleep—allows access to inner resources and facilitates both physical relaxation and mental clarity.

Breathing Techniques

Controlled breathing patterns that activate the parasympathetic nervous system, reducing stress and promoting a sense of calm.

Visualization

Guided imagery that taps into the mind's creative resources to promote healing, insight, and positive transformation.

Dynamic Relaxation

Simple physical movements paired with breath and awareness that release tension and increase body awareness.

Contemplation

Meditation practices that cultivate objective self-awareness and inner wisdom.
