Donation-Based Pricing Philosophy

Accessible Services for All

We believe in providing accessible services for those who need them; thus, payment for our consulting and educational services is entirely donation-based. These services are conducted remotely and can be done by phone or video.

At R.E.L., we believe Harm Reduction is about meeting the patients where they are, finding out what therapies they are interested in, and what works for them. Access is also a massive priority for us. Not just availability--access. Access to information, especially on alternative healing methods, should not have a price tag.

Therefore, our consultation services have no set fee--they are entirely donation-based. There is also no minimum donation to speak with me. Simply contribute whatever your current situation allows, and the universe will take care of the rest!

Consultation Process

Onboarding

Our aim with our onboarding process is to ascertain a client's risk factors for psychedelic therapy (if there are any), and to determine the type of support a client may need outside of what R.E.L. can provide, including references to other professionals if a client's situation appears beyond the abilities and outside the intended scope of our Harm Reduction services.

So, after you've found a time that works best for a remote Harm Reduction Consultation with Mike, you'll receive a few items in your email to fill out beforehand.

The information asked for in these documents is intended to ensure the safety and well-being of individuals interested in participating in psychedelic experiences. They'll provide us with a comprehensive understanding of your medical history, mental health status, substance use patterns, goals, and current state of mind.

It is important to be 100% completely honest with all of the answers, as it helps to weigh any potential risks or concerns before proceeding with a psychedelic session.

Consultation Session

During your session with Mike, you'll have the opportunity to discuss your specific questions, concerns, and goals related to psychedelics. Sessions typically last 60-90 minutes and can cover a range of topics:

  • Information about specific psychedelic substances
  • Research evidence for particular conditions
  • Harm reduction practices
  • Integration techniques
  • Legal considerations
  • Medical concerns or interactions

Follow-up Resources

After your consultation, Mike may provide additional resources, reading materials, or referrals based on your specific needs and interests. Many clients find that a single comprehensive session gives them the information they need, while others may choose to schedule follow-up consultations to discuss their ongoing journey.

Important Note

These consulting services are not intended to replace accurate medical advice or a medical diagnosis from a licensed medical professional. R.E.L.'s education and consulting services are not a substitute for medical advice or services, as provided by an appropriately licensed physician, psychiatrist, or similar. We aim to bridge the gap for folks who may not have immediate access to those services.
